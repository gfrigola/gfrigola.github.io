Here you have three of my favourite projects

UX RESEARCH & DESIGN

Music-Based Interval Training audio solution

We researched and designed an adaptative music-based heart rate feedback solution for sport practice in difficult terrains that demand of total visual attention. The new design has been compared with two other feedback solutions: the visual feedback from the sport watch display; and a familiar music solution from previous studies.

UX RESEARCH & SERVICE DESIGN

Solution for Valmet company on consumables tracking

We researched and designed a service solution for consumables tracking around the globe. The solution includes a tracking system for the whole journey of the consumable (from production site to use in the machine anywhere in the world), including storage at client-side and an app/desktop site to visualize, manage and communicate the data at both ends (Valmet and clients).

UX RESEARCH & SERIVCE DESIGN

Solution for Kone’s company packaging waste

We won a design event where we delivered a solution for Kone’s need to “do something” with their packaging material waste. We designed a solution based on Kone’s needs and values that could report benefit not only for Kone itself, but for society.
